Services

Dental Cleaning

Removes plaque and tartar to prevent cavities and gum disease, maintaining a healthy and bright smile.

Teeth Whitening

Aesthetic treatment that lightens teeth, removing stains and discoloration for a brighter smile.

Cavity Repair

Restoration of teeth damaged by cavities with aesthetic materials that blend perfectly with the natural tooth colour.

Dental Alignment

Correction of misalignments and malocclusions with orthodontic appliances, enhancing the aesthetics and functionality of the smile.

Tooth Replacement

Fixed or removable solutions to restore missing teeth, improving chewing, speech, and the aesthetics of the smile.
